Paste the text of your poem into the left side, then click the button at the top. The first line of your text should be the title of the poem. The second line should contain the author's name. The third line should be blank, and the rest of the text should be arranged in stanzas (if your poem has them), separated by blank lines. When you're done, copy the text and save it as "poem.js" in the root directory of your

project
